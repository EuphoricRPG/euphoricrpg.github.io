About Euphoric

Euphoric is a love letter to the golden age of computer RPGs, inspired by the legendary Ultima series. Built with Godot Engine, it combines classic tile-based exploration, turn-based tactical combat, and deep character interactions in a sprawling fantasy world.

Wake on mysterious shores with fragments of memory. Explore vast islands, converse with NPCs using a keyword-based dialog system, engage in strategic grid-based combat, and uncover the truth of your past. Your choices will determine not only who you are, but the very future of this world and its people.
